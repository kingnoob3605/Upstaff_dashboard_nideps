Who is she

MEET
RIGBY

Rigby is a tabby cat from New York who took the entire internet hostage with one GIF. She's best known for sitting there, tongue hanging out, looking absolutely feral while what appears to be liquid sprays at the camera. Is she peeing? Is it water? The world may never know. The world does not care.

She is a special needs cat diagnosed with Fainting Goat Syndrome (Myotonia Congenita) — a condition that causes her muscles to stiffen involuntarily, giving her that permanently unhinged expression that the entire internet fell in love with.

Managed by the Masé Talent Group, Rigby has gone from zero to celebrity with merchandise, Cameo appearances, and more memes than most humans will ever achieve in a lifetime.

🔬 The science bit

HER
CONDITION

01🐐
Fainting Goat Syndrome
Officially called Myotonia Congenita — the same condition that makes fainting goats go stiff. Rigby's muscles lock up involuntarily, causing her signature frozen expressions and perpetual derp face.
02🧠
Neurologist Confirmed
Multiple vets just said "special needs" and called it a day. Her owner pushed harder, got a referral to a neurologist, who ran an EMG test — sedating Rigby and using muscle-reading probes. Finally cracked the case.
03👅
The Tongue Situation
That perpetually hanging tongue? Also a direct result of her muscle condition. It just stays out. Always. Eternally. She doesn't care. She has 2.5 million followers because of it.
04💪
Living Her Best Life
Don't feel bad for Rigby. She is thriving, beloved, talent-agented, and probably making more money than you. Her condition didn't slow her down — it made her an internet celebrity.
